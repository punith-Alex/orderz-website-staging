If your business runs on people more than systems, the cost is already showing.

Every frontline hire in Singapore costs you salary, levy, and training before they add value

A new service staff member in Singapore earns $1,800–$2,500 per month before CPF. Add foreign worker levy if applicable, uniform, and training time. If they leave in six months — which many do — you pay that cost again. Every role you automate away is a recurring monthly saving of $1,800 or more — often more than the software costs per year.

High staff turnover means you are always training someone new

The average Singapore F&B staff tenure is under 12 months. Every time someone leaves, a manager spends two to three weeks retraining a replacement — costing the business productivity and service consistency. OrderZ standardises order-taking, billing, and booking so a new staff member is operational within hours, not weeks.

Salons and clinics lose revenue to no-shows that staff manually chase

A salon with 8 bookings per day and a 15% no-show rate loses more than 1 appointment slot every single day. Staff spend time making reminder calls that customers often ignore anyway. OrderZ sends automated WhatsApp reminders before every appointment — reducing no-shows without requiring any staff time.

What Singapore businesses see after running on OrderZ.

Customers order themselves — your staff handle the kitchen and service, not order-taking

OrderZ QR ordering puts the menu on every table. Customers scan, browse, and order from their phone. The order goes directly to the kitchen display — no order-taker at the table, no notepad, no keying into the POS. A 50-seat restaurant can run a full lunch service with two fewer front-of-house staff without any drop in speed or accuracy.

Appointments book themselves 24/7 — with automatic reminders, no staff involvement

Clients book from Instagram, WhatsApp, or your website at any time of day. Confirmation goes out automatically. Reminders go out 24 hours before the appointment. Reschedules re-open the slot automatically. Your front desk staff handle clients who are already in the salon — not the incoming booking queue.

Billing closes in seconds — one less person managing the cashier function every shift

New staff are operational in hours — your business no longer depends on individuals

When your processes are in OrderZ, a new staff member follows the system — not a colleague who happens to know how things work. Order flow, payment, package redemption, and appointment management are all guided by the software. Turnover stops costing you weeks of productivity loss.
