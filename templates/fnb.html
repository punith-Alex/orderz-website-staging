Peak hour is where the money is — and where everything breaks

Tables filling up should be your best-case scenario. Instead, orders are getting mixed up, waitstaff are relaying instructions across a loud kitchen, and your best customers are watching the chaos unfold. Without a system that connects floor to kitchen instantly, peak hour is the moment every gap in your operation becomes visible to the people you most need to impress.

A wrong dish, a missed modifier, a forgotten item — each one is a small moment of broken trust. In a market where every FnB business is competing for loyal regulars, a single bad experience drives customers to the next option. Multiple disconnected systems mean your team is doing the same work twice

POS on one system. Online orders on a tablet. Inventory in a spreadsheet. Delivery on a third platform. Every time staff enter data into more than one place, the chance of error multiplies. One platform that handles everything is the only way to run a clean operation.

Frequently asked questions about OrderZ for FnB businesses.

What types of FnB businesses does OrderZ support?
OrderZ supports the full range of Singapore FnB business types — restaurants, hawker stalls, cafes, food courts, QSR operations, casual dining, small chains, and bars and pubs. The platform handles both counter service and full table service, and scales from a single-stall hawker setup to a multi-outlet chain without requiring a separate system at each stage of growth.
Can OrderZ handle dine-in and takeaway orders on the same system?
Yes. OrderZ manages dine-in table orders, takeaway counter orders, and delivery orders in one unified platform. All three order types flow to the same kitchen display and are captured in the same reporting dashboard — so there is no need to reconcile data across separate systems or manually transfer orders between platforms at the end of each service.
Does OrderZ work for hawker stalls that do not have table service?
Yes. OrderZ is designed for single-person counter operations as much as for full restaurant setups. For hawker stalls, the system supports fast counter POS with QR payment, receipt printing, and real-time sales reporting — without requiring table management, multiple staff, or complex configuration. Most single-operator stalls are live within one working day.
How quickly can an FnB business be set up on OrderZ?
Most FnB businesses are live on OrderZ within one to three working days. Setup includes menu configuration, POS terminal setup, kitchen display installation where required, and full staff training — all completed by the local Singapore support team. There is no lengthy IT implementation or technical knowledge required from the business owner.
Can I run QR table ordering and a counter POS at the same time?
Yes. QR table ordering and counter POS operate simultaneously on OrderZ — all orders from both channels fire to the same kitchen display and are tracked in the same live dashboard. This is standard for restaurants that want to offer table self-ordering while retaining a counter option for walk-in and takeaway customers.
Does OrderZ integrate with GrabFood and Foodpanda?
OrderZ includes a direct online ordering channel for delivery and takeaway — commission-free, through your own branded page, synced to your POS. For integration with third-party delivery platforms such as GrabFood or Foodpanda, speak to the OrderZ team about integration options currently available for your specific setup and outlet type.
Can I manage multiple FnB outlets from one dashboard?
Yes. OrderZ multi-outlet management lets you view revenue, menu performance, staff activity, and stock levels across all locations from a single login. You can update menus centrally, compare outlet performance side by side, and set outlet-specific pricing — without logging into each location separately or manually consolidating end-of-week reports.
Is OrderZ billing GST-compliant?
Yes. OrderZ generates GST-inclusive receipts automatically for every transaction. Tax rates are configurable, and all billing records are stored and exportable for accounting purposes. The system is built for Singapore GST requirements and is used by registered FnB businesses across the island.
Does OrderZ support kitchen display screens?
Yes. Kitchen display screens are a core component of OrderZ. Every order placed — whether via QR table menu, counter POS, or online ordering — fires to the kitchen display the instant it is submitted. Screens can be configured by station, and orders can be marked complete with a single tap to keep kitchen workflow moving at pace.
What happens to my data if I expand from one outlet to multiple outlets?
Your data is centralised in OrderZ from the moment you open your first outlet. When additional locations are added to the account, your existing menu, customer, and reporting data carries across automatically. There is no migration or re-setup required, and outlet performance comparisons are available from the first day each new location goes live.
